Membership

Individual Memberships

Individual memberships are $100 per year for faculty and staff, and $10 per year for students. Please get in touch with us at glimprint.org@gmail.com if you want to become a member but do not have the funds - we will work with you.

Membership Benefits:

  • Featured profile on the GLIMPRINT website
  • Access to an expertise matrix of GLIMPRINT scientists to develop new collaborations
  • Announcements of new funding opportunities, workshops, and other events
  • Ability to apply for travel stipends
  • Discounts to workshops and GLIMPRINT events
